Parking lot management system

Our website here will help you manage your parking lot in an easy way.

Are you an owner of a shop struggling to manage the vehicles being parked in your parking lot?
You're here at the right place!! Welcome to the parking lot management system, where you, as the owner of a shop with a parking lot, can efficently make use of this software to manage the vehicles that line up at your shop. You can set the rate that you charge per hour of the vehicle that is being parked, generate bills when the vehicle leaves the lot, keep track of the slots available in your parking, and so on! So get registered and start parking your customer's vehicles efficiently!! Why automate parking?

Why did we come up with this idea?

Since managing the parking lot manually is a tedious job, implementing a parking lot management system for transaction
management, searching for a particular vehicle using the license plate, availability of slots etc. We decided to generalize
it and allow the manager of the parking lot to login to the system and personalize the rates /modify them in the future.

What all features does this system have?

You have to Sign up to access the system's features. Upon signing in, you will be given a lot of options ranging from adding a vehicle to generating the bill. To add a particular vehicle to the lot, click on the "Add vehicles" button and you can enter the vehicle's license plate number by using the image of the license plate and using our image sensing technology the license plate number is automatically read into the system. The system will give you details about the vehicle : Parked slot, owner name and entry time. For generating the bill again choose the license plate of the leaving vehicle and the bill will be generated according to the rate set by the manager.
